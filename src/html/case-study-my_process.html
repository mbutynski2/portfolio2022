My Process

Defining the DXC UX/UI Capability

  • Role: UX/UI Capability Lead
  • Timeframe:2 yrs
  • Team:6UX, 12UI
  • Company:DXC

Overview

As UX/UI capability lead, I led the creation of our DXC UX process within the New Orleans Digital Transformation Center. This allowed our team to align on common practices to become unified in our method. The goal was to evangelize the UX process both internally and externally to gain awareness and secure projects for the team.

UX Definition

We visually design digital experiences based on a deep understanding of the users and the business strategic objectives. We validate these designs through usability testing and iterate based on user feedback.

DXC UX Process

Discover- The initial phase of our process of discovery, we focus on understanding the needs of the users. We also aid in the defining of the problem using design thinking methodology.

Design- We solution based on the painpoints discovered and present many variations of wireframes to the stakeholders working with the development team. We get stakeholder approvals on high fidelity wireframes before handoff to development.

Develop & Deploy- We then assist the build of the designs. We work alongside development in asset creation and oversee the UI design quality. We also use this time to validate assumptions with users.

Step 1

Discover

Empathize

  • Initiate client interviews
  • Understand requirements
  • Create user personas
  • Collaborate on user stories

Define

  • Analyze competitors
  • Solution based on latest UX trends

Step 2

Design

  • Evaluate and re-draw

Design

  • Create prototypes
  • Define UX guidelines for developers

Step 3 & 4

Develope & Deploy

Sketch

  • Implement
  • Aid in implementing functionality
  • Assist in build experience

Evaluate

  • Perform usability testing & surveys
  • Create audit reports
  • Identify improvements
