Overview

Our CTMS application within our Clinical Domain had client driven requests to enhance our current risk management offering to meet the regulatory guidelines outlined by the ICH E6.

  • Deep Dive ICH E6 - This requirement was established to improve subject safety and data integrity.

  • Transformation - Platform team to build out this component as the first reusable mirco-services platform.

Goal

Provide a flexible platform to detect, manage, and report study risks that meet the mandatory ICH E6 guidelines and enhance the user experience within a clinical trial.

Feedback

Efforts to understand our users current needs and challenges. Here are a few:

  • Group Risks by Category - As a Functional Area/Domain Manager area user, it is critical for them to focus on risks associated with their domain. The Study Manager would want to be able to see all of the risks across all domains.

  • Avoid Essays - To prevent "essays", limit the characters in the risk description.

  • Baseline - Establish current state of offering and perform high level heuristic evaluation.

  • More Guidance - Tooltip that explains how the risk score is calculated is required. Also, guidance on how to write a risk.
