Innovation Tracker

Manufactoring & Automotive Tool

  • Role: UX Design Lead
  • Timeframe:4 Weeks
  • Team: UX, SA, SM, BA, 3SE
  • Company:DXC

Overview

A multinational tire manufacturing company would like a way for their employees to submit company-specific innovation ideas. This tool would allow stakeholders to gather insights, and track the process. In my role as UX Design Lead, I led discovery, design, and assisted development. I also presented to stakeholders.

Goal

The goal is to provide a functioning MVP in four weeks to allow employees, executives, and DXC leadership to submit innovations, gather insights, and track the process. This tool would increase our partner revenue by providing DXC with incentivized employee led actionable concepts to test.

Process

Creative during COVID

Coronavirus stay at home order made it essential to be creative with how to collaborate using video to define user stories.

I Heart Figma

After kickoff meeting and review of requirements with the client, I quickly jumped into wireframes to allow for future meetings to center around visual solutions. This focuses the team on functionality and defining scope.

We utilized Figma's ability to co-collaborate with the client and development team to iron out details of the low-fidelity wireframes during week one.

Design by committee

Next week, we solidified the high-fidelity designs with the team so they could begin to develop. They had been developing already based on the low-fidelity designs.

Beginning of week 3 we had a very successful demo to the client that involved 3 screens and basic functionality working on the local development environment.

Sprint to the finish

End of week 3, we had more screens to demo. Throughout the build, I provided guidance to the development team via screenshots. There were less and less revisions each time.

Development was complete by end of day Monday at the beginning of week 4. We had time to perfect and even code a few stretch goals to wow the client.

Results

Demo to leadership and account team went smoothly. By deploying this tool, we increased customer’s visibility into DXC’s innovations from the results of the tracking process. We enable customers to measure DXC’s innovation capability with these metrices, one vehicle being the NPS. We increased DXC’s NPS especially in the area of innovation which enables customers to recommend DXC based on the ability to generate new ideas and develop creative ways to resolving business problems.
